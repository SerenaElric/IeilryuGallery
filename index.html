With a love for storytelling and a vivid imagination, I transforms ideas into mesmerizing visual art that transports viewers to enchanting worlds. My journey into the realm of fantasy art began at an early age, inspired by classic literature, epic films, and the beauty of nature.

 

About Me

Hey there! My name is Karolina/Isvoc, a creature designer and a fantasy illustrator. I'm a freelance artist, past clients include work with 3dTotal, ArenaNet, Good Smile Company, Alchemy Art Group and more. Currently I'm focused on private commissions but I'm always interested in new projects! I'm rather loud and passionate about art so if you feel like chatting and joining our community, feel free to check out my discord server! Privately I really enjoy reading, gardening, knitting, disappearing in the woods and getting obsessed.
